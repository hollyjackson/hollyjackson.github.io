Conwayâ€™s Game of Life is a cellular automaton that was created by John Conway. Through creating this game, I learned the basics of making an evolutionary algorithm.

The game follows the rules below (defined here):

  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors survives.

  • Any dead cell with three live neighbors becomes a live cell.

  • All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead cells stay dead.
